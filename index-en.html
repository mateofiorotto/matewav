Work Methodology

Contact

1. Contact and Details

We will get in touch to discuss the details of your project. Here we will define the goals and, if possible, I will provide you with advice.

Proposal

2. Proposal and Approval

I will provide you with a detailed proposal outlining the work plan and budget. If you agree, we proceed to the third step.

Payment

3. Initial Payment

A 50% deposit is required to proceed (no refunds).

Work in Progress

4. First Part of the Work

I will start with the editing of the video/animation or other service previously discussed.

Review

5. Review and Feedback

A preview of the half-completed project will be provided, and corrections will be made as needed.

Completion

6. Project Completion

Once reviewed, I will begin completing the project.

Second Review

7. Second Review

A second review will be provided before the final delivery.

Delivery

8. Final Delivery

The final video will be delivered without a watermark and with the agreed-upon duration.

No Refunds

9. No Refunds

It is important to note that no refunds will be made at any stage of the process once the payment has been made.
